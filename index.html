Rhesus Macaques

One of the sly monkeys living in the Himalayan mountains.

  • Scientific Name: Macaca mulatta
  • Average Length: 53 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Nepal, Burma, Thailand, Afghanistan, Vietnam, southern China

Now I am on a three-week ski trip in the Indian Himalayas. I found hordes of wild monkeys living near places where it was usual to meet a pack of dogs. Despite the fact that on the snow lies they rummage in the garbage and beg for passers-by something tasty.
